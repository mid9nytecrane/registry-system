# Product Overview

**Political Registry** is a web application for managing polling station attendance and membership at the community level. It is built for the Damongo Community Information Center and targets political/electoral administration use cases.

## Core Purpose

- Track attendance at polling stations
- Manage member/voter records
- Support staff (admin) and regular member roles
- Provide a simple, accessible interface for station-level data entry and lookup

## Target Users

- **Administrators (staff)**: Full access including an admin dashboard, member management, and station oversight
- **Members**: Limited access to view station and membership information

## Key Concepts

- **Polling Stations**: Physical locations being tracked
- **Members**: People registered in the system, associated with stations
- **Attendance**: Records linking members to stations at a point in time
